Tim Berners-Lee is a software engineer who invented internet(or world wide web) in 1989 while at CERN the European particle physics laboratory. He is the directoor of the World Wide Web Consortium that he founded and a proffesor in the Computer science Department at unversity of Oxford. In 2001 he became the Fellow of the Royal family and in 2001 he was knighted .

In 1994 Berners-Lee founded the WOrld Wide Web Consortium (W3c) at the laboratory of computer science (LCS) at the Massachuetts Institute of Technology in Boston . It was made up of companies that work together to create web standards and also improve the web. It was quickly agreed that the technology should be free from royalties and patents . Berner Lee has served as director of W3C since it was found.
